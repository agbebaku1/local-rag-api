ACME Company HR Policy Document

Vacation Policy:
Employees receive 15 vacation days per year.
New employees must complete 90 days before taking vacation.
Unused vacation days roll over up to a maximum of 10 days.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Remote work must be approved by direct manager.
All remote employees must be available during core hours 10am-3pm EST.

Health Benefits:
The company covers 80% of health insurance premiums.
Dental and vision coverage is included.
Benefits begin on the first day of employment.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must treat all colleagues with respect.
Harassment of any kind will result in immediate termination.
All conflicts should be reported to HR within 24 hours.
